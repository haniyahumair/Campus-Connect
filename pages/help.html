Help / User Manual

Welcome to CampusConnect! Below is a quick guide to help you get started with creating events, browsing listings, and managing your account

How to use CampusConnect

  1. Create an account: Click the "Sign Up" button and enter your university email address and password.
  2. Create and event:Once logged in, navigate to your dashboard and click "Create Event." Fill in the event details including date, price, and category.
  3. Browse events and view details:Go to the Events page, browse the list, and click "View Details" on any event.
  4. Add an event to the cart:On the event details page, click "Add to Cart" to reserve your spot.
  5. Checkout:Go to your cart, review your selected events. Complete the checkout process using any available payment method as a payment system has not been integrated.
  6. View your profile:Click on "Profile" in the navigation menu. Here you can see all events you have created and all events you are attending.
  7. Save events:While browsing events, click the heart icon on any event to save it to your favourites list for later.
  8. Notifications:Click the bell icon located in the navigation bar to receive and view any notifications from the admin regarding your events.

How to become and Admin

Admin access is available only to verified university staff and student society leaders. To request admin privileges, please email your CV and a brief explanation of your role to:

📧 campusconnect.admin@university.edu

Once your request is reviewed and approved, you will receive admin access to create and manage events on behalf of your society or department.
